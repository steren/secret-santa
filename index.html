Secret Santa add-on for Google sheet

Secret Santa helps you organise a blind gift distribution among your family, your mates or even your work team. A draw assigns who everyone has to offer a gift to. Everybody gets the same amount of gifts, but nobody knows who it is from.

The Secret Santa add-on allows you to specify participants who should not offer presents to each other.

Privacy Policy

Secret Santa add-on for Google sheet does collect any personal information. The names and email addresses are processed by the add-on, but never stored anywhere else than in the current Sheet.

When installing the add-on, you have to authorize it to:

  • Access Sheet in order for the add-on to read your current sheet.
  • Send emails in order for the add-on to send emails to the participants of the secret santa.
